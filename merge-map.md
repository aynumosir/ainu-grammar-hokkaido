## Part reordering (old part → new position)

Dependency-driven: affixal verb morphology stays before syntax, but the periphrastic/nominalization-based OPERATOR systems (TAM, Evidentiality, Negation) move to AFTER the simple clause and nominalization.

- The Verb (structure/transitivity/verbal number) and the Personal-Affix System SWAP, so verb-internal morphology precedes the affix system.
- The Simple Clause and Nonverbal Predication and Nominalization/Relativization/Complementation move UP, ahead of TAM, Evidentiality, and Negation.
- Within the Simple Clause part, constituent-order and argument-realization chapters move to the FRONT.
- Within Phonology, syllable structure / phonotactics precede the glide/hiatus/diphthong chapter.
- Within Word Classes, the property-concept (no-adjective) chapter sits adjacent to the verbal-subclass chapter.
